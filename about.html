Raised in Appalacia on a strict diet of unfettered imagination (and too much late night TV), Jordan Tynan has always been fascinated with fantasy and the human element which underscores all circumstances we face. He holds a Bachelors degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of South Carolina, and a Master's in Electrical Engineering from NC State University. Electrical engineering requires a love for the invisible and an imagination for the imaginary, so Jordan tries to channel that into worlds at the end of your fingertips with power systems that beg to be explored.

In the rare moments when Jordan is not writing, you can find him trying to dig out of his backlog of movies and video games. He claims to be a decent chef, is a mediocre distance runner, and has a phenomenal appetite. He likes to run more than he cooks, cook more than he eats, and eat as much as possible.
